I'm a UI Designer with 4+ years of experience designing visually engaging and user-friendly digital products across web and mobile platforms.

I design clean, intuitive, and high-converting interfaces that improve user experience and drive better engagement. With a strong background in graphic and visual design, I bring a keen eye for aesthetics, branding, and detail into every product I work on.

Having worked in startup environments, I've been involved in building and improving products from the ground up — adapting quickly, solving problems, and contributing across multiple areas of design.

While UI design is my core strength, I also apply UX principles such as wireframing, user flows, and research to ensure smooth and effective user journeys. My work has contributed to improving usability and conversions by simplifying complex interactions and creating frictionless experiences.

I have experience working in Agile teams, collaborating closely with developers and stakeholders to deliver scalable, consistent, and visually strong products.
